Griddy Quest

What is Griddy Quest?

Griddy Quest will be a tile-matching puzzle game with some RPG elements. Play as Kwesi and Phea as they traverse through a fantasy world where combat is represented as a puzzle game. The main objective of this puzzle game is to clear pieces by swapping them to match three or more of them in a line. Unique mechanics will be introduced as the player progresses. Players will also be able to walk around in various hand-crafted top-down maps and interact with NPCs. Players might want to avoid these NPCs however, as they will likely want to trigger combat with the player.
